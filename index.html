Portland's Own Dance Troupe!

Kazüm has frequently worked with the March 4th Marching Band ,The Oregon Country Fair and Wanderlust Circus. Kazüm has established themselves as an integral part of Portland's art community, and continues define the role of local circus in Portland's future.
